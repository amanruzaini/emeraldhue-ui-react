# Badge

EH includes two badge families: **BadgeNonSemantic** for general labelling and **BadgeSemantic** for status communication.

## Design tokens used

- **Root element**: `<span>` (inline for text flows)
- **Typography**: Non-standard composite — `text-[length:var(--eh-font-size-body-sm)]` with `leading-[var(--eh-font-line-height-body-xs)]`
- **Non-semantic colours**: 6 compound variants for appearance × colour
- **Semantic colours**: 8 compound variants for appearance × status
